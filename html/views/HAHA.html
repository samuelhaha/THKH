About Us

Ang Mo Kio – Thye Hua Kwan Hospital (AMK – THKH) is a 360-bedded community hospital that offers
rehabilitative and sub-acute care. It offers customised care plan for every patient,
to help them regain independence and mobility, so as to integrate back into the community.
